About FPNO Skills Hub

Empowering Future Leaders Through Skills Development

Our Story

Established as a cornerstone of Federal Polytechnic Nekede's commitment to practical education, the FPNO Skills Acquisition Hub has been at the forefront of vocational and technical training since 2020. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical industry skills, preparing students for successful careers in an ever-evolving global marketplace.

Today, we proudly serve as a center of excellence in skills development, offering comprehensive training programs in digital technology, vocational skills, and entrepreneurship. Our state-of-the-art facilities and industry-experienced instructors ensure that every student receives the highest quality practical education.
